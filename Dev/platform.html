NVIDIA GB200 and GB300 only — Blackwell-generation hardware from the start. No H100, no legacy chips. Early access customers will be consulted on the right configuration for their workload before go-live.
Antriksh is AI-native from the ground up — 10× denser racks, liquid cooling that prevents GPU throttling, 40–50% lower pricing, INR billing, zero egress fees, and true data sovereignty with no cross-border flows, ever. Foreign hyperscalers are general-purpose clouds that added GPUs. We are a GPU-first company that is building India's compute stack.
Your data never leaves India — by architecture, not by policy. Full tenant isolation, encryption at rest and in transit, air-gapped zones for regulated industries. Zero staff access to your data without your explicit authorisation. We are designed for DPDP Act compliance, CERT-In standards, and RBI data localisation requirements.
Yes. Early access customers get a trial allocation to benchmark their workloads before signing any contract. We want you to verify the performance numbers yourself — run your actual training job and see the cost and latency difference first-hand.
8 GPUs minimum for on-demand workloads. There is no maximum — scale to 1,024 GPUs in a single cluster. Enterprise customers can reserve larger dedicated blocks. All provisioned via API or dashboard in under 15 minutes.
We permanently reserve 10% of our capacity for AI startups we incubate. Credits range from $50K to $2M in GPU compute, allocated based on your stage and compute requirements. There is no equity ask for the credits themselves — we participate in your upside through the incubation programme separately. Apply via the waitlist with a note about your company.
Full CUDA compatibility — PyTorch, JAX, TensorFlow, vLLM, TensorRT, Triton, HuggingFace, DeepSpeed, Megatron-LM, and anything that runs on NVIDIA hardware. We do not restrict your software stack. Bring your own Docker image or use our base images. NVLink and InfiniBand are fully exposed so multi-node frameworks like PyTorch DDP and FSDP work natively.
Enterprise and reserved contracts include 99.9% uptime SLAs with financial credits for downtime. On-demand workloads are best-effort with typical availability above 99.5%. We have dual power feeds from two independent sources, N+2 cooling redundancy, and 72-hour diesel backup — designed to the same reliability standards as hyperscale facilities.
