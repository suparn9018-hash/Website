Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Antriksh different from existing data centers?

Unlike traditional data centers built for web hosting, Antriksh is AI-native with liquid cooling supporting 150kW+ per rack (vs 20-30kW industry standard), specifically designed for GPU clusters and AI workloads.

Why does India need its own AI infrastructure?

Data sovereignty laws, 200ms+ latency from foreign servers, 3-4x higher costs, and security concerns make domestic AI infrastructure critical for India's digital economy.

How does the Venture Compute program work?

AI startups can exchange 5-10% equity for GPU credits, getting access to expensive compute resources without upfront capital, while Antriksh builds a portfolio of high-growth AI companies.
