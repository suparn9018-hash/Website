GPU Sizing Guide

How many GPUs does your model need?

Training needs 9× more memory than inference. Reasoning models fall in between. Here's the quick math.

The formulas
Training
Params × 18 × 1.25 ÷ VRAM
Weights + gradients + optimizer = ~18 bytes/param
Inference
Params × 2 × 1.25 ÷ VRAM
Weights only = 2 bytes/param in FP16
Extended Thinking
Params × ~4 × 1.25 ÷ VRAM
Weights + large KV cache from thinking tokens
